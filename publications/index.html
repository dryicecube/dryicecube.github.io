  1. Quantum Money Using Differential Phase Encoding
    Ashutosh Kumar Singh, Nilesh Sharma, Vaibhav Pratap Singh, Anil Prabhakar, and Sridhar Tayur
    In Optica Quantum 2.0 Conference and Exhibition, 2025
    Abs DOI

    A Quantum Money scheme is presented with preliminary experimental results. We show a three-party system with the Bank as the trustworthy node using differential phase encoding on weak coherent source to realise the transaction.

  2. A Novel and Agile Trusted Relay Scheme for Long Distance QKD Networks
    Vaibhav Pratap Singh, Ashutosh Kumar Singh, and Anil Prabhakar
    In Optica Quantum 2.0 Conference and Exhibition, 2025
    Abs DOI

    A novel trusted relay scheme is presented for long-distance QKD, focusing on reduced hardware and computational requirements, scalability, execution time, etc. The scheme works under a centralized control architecture like QKD-SDN controllers.
